The Basics of Stock Markets


Stocks are basically the shares of ownership in a company and stockholders have a fractional ownership of the company.
Stocks are typically listed as line graphs and they basically keep track of the stock price of the company overtime. It can show
the companies records by daily, monthly, and yearly.

How Should I Start Investing?

The best way to start is to do paper trading which will allow users
to buy and sell on a simulator without having to invest real money
that way the user is practicing before they decide to invest real
money. Paper trading is possible through opening a brokerage account
which is the quickest method of buying stocks.
