About Us

Founded in 2022 the Stocksim team are dedicated to helping users that are new
to the stock market industry but are looking to start somewhere and want to
learn


Unlike our competitors we want to provide an easy to use
website that can help new users with making the right
investments that best fits their needs. Stocksim will
show users the different options they can choose.

Users may refer to our information page which will provide
the educational information they will need to know
