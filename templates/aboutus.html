About Us

The Company

Founded in 2022 the Stocksim team are dedicated to helping users that are new to the stock market industry but are looking to start somewhere and want to learn

Our Goal

Unlike our competitors we want to provide an easy to use website that can help new users with making the right investments that best fits their needs. Stocksim will show users the different options they can choose. Users may refer to our information page which will provide the educational information they will need to know

The Team

We are team of software engineers who also invest in stocks back when we started making investments in other sites such as robinhood, we were not sure where to begin but this gave us the motivation to help others who are in the same positionas us by creating our own stock investment site.
